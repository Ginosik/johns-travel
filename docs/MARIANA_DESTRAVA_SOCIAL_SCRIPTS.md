# Mariana Destrava Lesson 1 Social Scripts

Source lesson: **Can you repeat that?**
Audience: Brazilian adults who understand English but freeze when they need to answer.
Core promise: one rescue phrase can keep the conversation alive.
CTA: **Book a private class** through the Mariana Destrava WhatsApp link.

## Instagram Reel / TikTok - 20 to 30 seconds

**Hook on screen:** Na hora de responder em ingles, da branco?

**Beat 1 - Setup**
Mariana entende a pergunta, mas ela vem rapido demais: "What did you do after work yesterday?"

**Beat 2 - Freeze moment**
Thought caption: "Eu entendi o comeco, mas o resto veio rapido demais."

**Beat 3 - Rescue phrase**
Show Answer Builder options:
- Repeat, please.
- Can you repeat that, please?
- Sorry, can you repeat that?

**Beat 4 - Coaching**
Voiceover: "A frase mais natural aqui e: Sorry, can you repeat that? Ela ganha tempo sem sair da conversa."

**Beat 5 - Practice prompt**
On screen: Say it out loud 3 times.
Line: "Sorry, can you repeat that?"

**CTA**
On screen: Quer destravar respostas simples em ingles? Book a private class.
