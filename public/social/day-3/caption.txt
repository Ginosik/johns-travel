Dia 3 - O ônibus errado para a praia certa: John pega o ônibus errado e transforma o erro em uma história de praia.
Cena do dia: John tenta ir para a Joaquina, pega o ônibus errado e acaba em Campeche.
Trecho da conversa em inglês:
John: "Today I tried to go to Joaquina, but I got on the wrong bus and ended up in Campeche instead."
Nicky: "Wait, you took the wrong bus and still found a beach? That sounds like a very lucky mistake."
John: "It was lucky after the panic passed. At first, I looked out the window and realized the signs did not match my map."
Frase para praticar: "That sounds like a very lucky mistake."
Vocabulário do dia: bus = ônibus, wrong = errado / errada, beach = praia, lost = perdido, market = mercado / mercadinho.
